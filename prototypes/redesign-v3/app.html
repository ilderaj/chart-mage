About ChartMage

ChartMage

ChartMage is a local-first Mermaid diagram editor. Diagrams render entirely in your browser using the Mermaid library. Nothing is sent to any server.

Forked from heyjunlin/chart-mage
Mermaid version: 10
Storage: localStorage (browser)
ChartMage

Better on desktop

The editor is designed for keyboard + large screen.
Open ChartMage on a laptop or desktop for the best experience.
