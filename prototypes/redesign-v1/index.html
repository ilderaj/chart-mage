03 — Features

Three cards, one promise

Replaces the current GIF triplet with restrained iconography and a fixed grid.

Layout solves itself

Type your actors and messages — ChartMage handles spacing, alignment, and arrow routing without a single click.

Predictive autocomplete

Inline hints know your participants, your loops, and your last arrow. Tab completes, Enter commits, Esc dismisses.

Syntax you read out loud

User ->> API: checkout says exactly what it does. Learnable in ten seconds, memorable forever.

A clean canvas, not a clean slate.

Charts are saved locally, exported as crisp PNGs, and stay version-friendly because they're just text. Paste into a Notion doc, a PR, or a Slack thread.
